About Us

Hart Eyes is a community for artists from beginners to professionals. However, what makes stand out more from the digital world is how this community will focus on the traditonal art world. Meaning, we showcase our traditional art, whether it be from sketches to full line work. Here we will share myst know tips and helpful advice on how to bring your traditoanl sketches to life! Though we may not be "official" professionals, we all have at least 10+ years of experience to share with you. This is a safe community to share your work and receive plenty of constructive feedback from artists all over the world. Together, we can keep the traditional art world alive!
